Ancient Roots

Pune, with its ancient name 'Punyanagari,' has a history that traces back to the 8th century. It was the center of the Rashtrakuta Dynasty and later became the prime political base for the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji.

British Era

During the British Raj, Pune emerged as a significant cultural and educational hub. The city witnessed various social and political movements, including the famous Aga Khan Palace, which served as a pivotal landmark during India's struggle for independence.

Modern Pune

Today, Pune is a thriving metropolis that preserves its historical grandeur while embracing modernity. Its history has left a mark on its architecture, culture, and way of life.
