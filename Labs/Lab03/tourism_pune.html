Historical Landmarks

Pune boasts a treasure trove of historical sites, including the Aga Khan Palace, Shaniwar Wada, and the Osho Ashram. These landmarks provide insights into the city's past and architectural splendor.

Natural Beauty

Experience the lush landscapes around Pune with visits to Sinhagad Fort, Mulshi Lake, and nearby Western Ghats. The city is a gateway to outdoor adventures and scenic beauty.
